pdf-strip

PDF files often carry hidden information you didn't put there -- your name, when you created it, which software you used, and sometimes even your organisation. This tool quietly removes all of that before you share the file. Nothing is uploaded; it all happens in your browser. Strip /Info and XMP metadata from a PDF. Runs entirely in your browser via Pyodide + pypdf -- the file never leaves your machine.
